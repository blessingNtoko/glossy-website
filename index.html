  • About our company

    Glossy Glo & Interior Design is a progressive and dynamic, service oriented, interior & exterior design company capable of handling projects of various natures over the full spectrum of construction, airless spray painting and carpentry. Clients are kept informed , their needs are complied with, quality is ensured, and we strive to complete projects in a timely manner while sticking to their budget.

    Our objective is to offer a tripartite source of solutions for all your construction, airless spray painting, and carpentry related needs. We support our clients from the projects conception, through its implementation, and all the way to its completion. We aim to exceed our clients expectations through quality and professionalism while using innovative technology and cost-effective solutions.
